About Us

Founded in 2024, DANNA BEVERAGES is committed to delivering high-quality mineral water. Our water is sourced from natural springs and undergoes rigorous filtration processes, including sand filtering, to ensure purity and freshness.

Our Mission

At Danna Beverages, our mission is to provide Ugandans with the purest and healthiest water to support their everyday hydration needs.
